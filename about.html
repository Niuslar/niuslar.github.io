About Me

I'm Nicolás Uslar, an engineer with an MSc in Sustainable Energy Systems from Queen Mary, University of London.

After finishing my MSc I discovered the world of Embedded Systems, and since then, I have worked towards my goal of becoming an Embedded Engineer. After taking online courses and working on projects alongside more experienced Embedded Engineers, I am confident I now have enough experience to be a valuable contribution to any team.

The main reason I chose Embedded Systems as a career is because I truly enjoy solving problems and in the last 1+ years I have aquired many tools that help me do this efficiently.

Here is a list of my current skills:

  • Embedded C/C++
  • Python (Intermediate)
  • Communication Protocols (I2C, UART, SPI)
  • Git/Github
  • Soldering
  • Basic PCB design
  • Linux
  • STM32, ESP32, AVR
  • MQTT

If you want to know more about me, please feel free to send me an e-mail at niuslar@outlook.com or visit my Github which can be found at the bottom of this page.

Thanks for stopping by!
